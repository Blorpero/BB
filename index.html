Biography:

Mr. Barber is a seasoned management consultant, with major firm experience at Accenture and Booz Allen Hamilton in addition to substantial independent consulting work. His primary focus areas are growth strategy, international business development, and operational effectiveness.

A true expert in the global distribution of Film & TV content, Mr. Barber also spent 20 years working in Hollywood. He held VP roles at Universal Studios, 20th Century Fox, and Warner Bros, covering all major business lines including Film, Television, and Theme Parks.

Mr. Barber has extensive experience in China, where he represented Warner Bros. in a multi-year collaboration between Hollywood studios and China’s Film Bureau. Mr. Barber visited China and also hosted Chinese government delegations visiting Hollywood, and facilitated valuable connections between China and US entertainment companies.

Mr. Barber holds an MBA from The Wharton School of Business and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Texas. He started his career as a management consultant for global firms Accenture and Booz Allen & Hamilton, with Universal Studios as his client.
