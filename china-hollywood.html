China - Hollywood Business Development


Location-based Entertainment Development: Mr. Barber worked as a VP at Universal Studios headquarters during the development of Universal Studios global expansion across Asia and North America. He advised in the areas of IP licensing, attraction development (such as Jurassic Park), and the modularization of theme park environments to keep the guest experience fresh and increase repeat visits.

Film and TV global distribution: Mr. Barber distributed Film and TV content for Universal Studios, 20th Century Fox, Warner Bros. He covered all major markets in Asia, Europe, and North America. Distribution channels included theatrical exhibition, cable/broadcast, digital/streaming, and packaged goods (Blu-ray and DVD).
